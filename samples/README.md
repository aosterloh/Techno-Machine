# Sample Requirements

To recreate the Techno Playground, you need a set of high-quality .wav samples. Place them in the `/samples` directory.

### Required Drum Samples
The app expects the following default filenames:
- `Kick_1.wav`: Solid, punchy techno kick.
- `Kick_2.wav`: Alternative kick or sub-heavy kick.
- `Snare.wav`: Classic 909 or industrial snare.
- `Clap.wav`: Sharp, resonant clap.
- `HH1.wav`: Closed hi-hat.
- `HH2.wav`: Open hi-hat.
- `HH3.wav`: Percussive hi-hat or shaker.
- `Ride.wav`: Driving ride cymbal.

### Optional Samples
The app will automatically detect any additional `.wav` files in the `/samples` folder and make them available in the track dropdowns. Suggested additions:
- `VocalA.wav`, `VocalB.wav`: Short vocal stabs or phrases.
- `Ride2.wav`: Alternative ride for variation.
- `Clap2.wav`, `Clap3.wav`: Layered claps.

### Technical Specifications
- **Format**: .wav
- **Bit Depth**: 16-bit or 24-bit
- **Sample Rate**: 44.1kHz or 48kHz
- **Length**: Short one-shots (usually < 1 second)
